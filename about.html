Frequently Asked Questions

What sparked your interest in game development?

I tried building my own (very broken) games on my potato PC—and fell in love with creating things through code and design

How did you start learning?

I started as a self-taught developer—learning through trial and error—then formalized my skills by pursuing a degree in IT and working on personal projects.
